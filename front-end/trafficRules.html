Traffic Rules


First time bicycling in Malmo? Here are some rules and guidelines provied to help you under your cyklingtour.


Eqiupment


Helmet

If you are fifthteen years or younger, you are entitled to have a helmet on your bikeride.

Cycle lamp

When it is dark outside, you have to have cycle lamps, both in front of the bike and in the back.

Reflex

It is not necessary to have reflex, but it can save you. In darkness car drivers have trouble to see cyclists that wear darker colored clothes.

Bell

It is required to have a bell on the bike, otherwise you could get fines.

Brake

It is required to have brakes on the bike, otherwise you could get fines.


Road signs


Stop sign

By this sign every veichle has to stop, even if you are on a bicykle.

Sidewalk

On a sidewalk you are allowed to cykle, as long as it isn´t faster than the walking speed.

Crosswalk

You will come across this sign if you are bicycling in trafic. This simply means that people who are walking have admission before bicycles.

Sidewalk area

You are allowed to drive here, but you have to adapt your speed to the people who are walking.

cycklecrossing

Here cars have to wait until you have crossed the street.

Sidewalk and bicykle path

Next to this sign, the road is provided and diveded both for bicycle and persons who walk.

Disallowance of vehicle

This sign tells all veichle, including bicycles, that driving is not allowed.

Decreed sidewalk

This sign tells you that the road is a sidewalk and that you are not allowed to bicykle here.

Traffic lights

When red or yellow light, you have to stop. When the light turns green again, you are allowed to drive again.
