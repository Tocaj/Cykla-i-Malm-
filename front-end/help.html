Traffic Rules


First time bicycling in Malmo? Here are some rules and guidelines provied to help you under your cyklingtour.


Helmet

If you are fifthteen years or younger, you are entitled to have a helmet on your bikeride.


Stop sign

By this sign every veichle has to stop, even if you are on a bicykle.
